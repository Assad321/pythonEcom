Welcome to e-Grocery system

E-Grocery is a web based online application which provides a platform to buy fresh vegetables, fruits and dairy products. A wide range of category is available which will be provided at your door steps at any time with just some clicks. Not only it provides the facility of online shopping, but it also facilitates the service of grocery purchase according to nutrition and health requirement. Challenge yourself to eat more vegetables and fruits daily. Start with everyday favorites such as apples, green grapes, green beans and then add a new fruit to your shopping cart to try out its nutritional benefits.

Vitamins & Minerals in Rruit & Vegetables

Fruits and vegetables contain many vitamins and minerals that are good for your health. These include vitamins A (beta-carotene), C and E, magnesium, zinc, phosphorous and folic acid. Folic acid may reduce blood levels of homocysteine, a substance that may be a risk factor for coronary heart disease.
